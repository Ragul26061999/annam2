-----------------------------------------------------
Grouped List with Descriptions
-----------------------------------------------------

Core
- core.users: Application/authentication users
- core.persons: Shared demographics (patients & staff)
- core.patients: Patient master data (links to persons)
- core.staff: Staff master data (links to persons)
- core.staff_roles: Role catalog for staff
- core.departments: Hospital departments/units (OPD, ward, lab, pharmacy)
- core.facilities: Multi-hospital/branch data
- core.staff_schedules: Staff shifts/rosters

Clinical
- clinical.appointments: Scheduled consultations
- clinical.encounters: Actual clinical visits/episodes (OPD/ER/IPD)
- clinical.vitals: Vital signs per encounter
- clinical.allergies: Patient allergies
- clinical.symptoms: Symptoms recorded in an encounter
- clinical.medical_history: Past illnesses/conditions

Inpatient
- inpatient.admissions: Admission & discharge episodes
- inpatient.wards: Wards within a facility
- inpatient.beds: Bed master with type/status
- inpatient.bed_allocations: Bed assignment history

Lab
- clinical.lab_tests: Catalog of available tests
- clinical.lab_orders: Lab orders placed per encounter
- clinical.lab_order_items: Individual ordered tests
- clinical.lab_results: Results with values, units, and ranges

Pharmacy
- pharmacy.items: Medicine catalog (single source of truth)
- pharmacy.batches: Batch/expiry tracking
- pharmacy.inventory_txns: Stock ledger (GRN, issue, dispense, return)
- pharmacy.prescriptions: Prescriptions issued per encounter
- pharmacy.rx_lines: Prescription line items (dose, frequency, duration)
- pharmacy.dispenses: Records of dispensed quantities

Billing
- billing.fee_schedules: Billable services catalog
- billing.fee_rates: Rate per service (time-bound)
- billing.charges: Charges generated (labs, procedures, pharmacy, etc.)
- billing.invoices: Patient invoices (encounter/admission context)
- billing.invoice_lines: Line items in invoices
- billing.payments: Payment history against invoices

Ops / Reference
- ops.audit_log: Immutable change log (insert/update/delete)
- ops.attachments: Documents/images linked to records
- ref.codes: Controlled vocabularies (diagnoses, tests, procedures)
